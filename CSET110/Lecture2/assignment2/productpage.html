Airpod Maxes


The AirPods Max are Apple's high-end over-ear headphones, featuring:

  • Design:
    • Sleek stainless steel frame with memory foam ear cushions and contoured aluminum ear cups.
    • Available in multiple colors.

  • Features:
    • Active Noise Cancellation
    • Transparency mode
    • Spatial audio with dynamic head tracking
    • Digital Crown for controls

  • Technology:
    • Dual H1 chips for computational audio
    • High-fidelity sound

  • Battery Life:
    • Up to 20 hours of listening with ANC
    • Spatial audio enabled
    • With a Smart Case for battery preservation
These headphones deliver premium sound quality and comfort, combining advanced technology with elegant design.
